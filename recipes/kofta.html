Baked Kofta

What is Baked Kofta?

Kafta bil Sanieh is a delicious Falastini baked casserole made with layers of ground meat patties, potatoes, onions and tomatoes in a spiced tomato sauce. Best served with rice, labneh, fresh pita bread and fattoush.

Ingredients

for Kofta mix:
  • 1.5 Ground Beef
  • 1 tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • 1 tablespoon Allspice, ground
  • 2 teaspoon Garlic Powder
  • 1 teaspoon Smoked Paprika
  • Salt & Pepper to preference
Tomato Sauce
  • 1 cup Italian Parsley
  • 1/2 Red Bell Pepper
  • 1 Jalapenominced
  • 1 Yellow Onion medium
  • 4 garlic cloves minced
Layering
  • two onions sliced
  • three tomatoes sliced
  • three potatoessliced

Instrutions

Preheat the oven to 375F.

In the food processor, pulse all the ingredients for the sauce until fine. You don't want to over-pulse to where the mixture is wet but just enough until it is fine.

Add this to the ground beef. Season the beef with salt, pepper, allspice, garlic powder, tomato paste, and smoked paprika.

Mix just enough to where the components are well combined.

Add the beef to an 11” x 17” baking dish and spread out evenly.

Top this off with a flat layer of tomatoes and onions. Season with salt and pepper

Bake in a preheated 375F oven for 40 min covered with foil. At this point, the kofta will have released excess liquid

Enjoy with a side of rice, salad, and yogurt and possibly a pita
